Instructions

Game is very simple, you have a 'hero' and you have two various types of enemies, skeletons and cloacked dudes. Move with the arrow keys and any other key board button will make the hero use his sword.

With one swipe you will kill a skeleton but it will take a couple of swipes to kill the enemy. To advance to the next level you must kill all of the enemies present and collect the key. Note you don't have to kill all the skeletons. The number of enemies will increase with each level. If you kill all of the skeletons you will receive a heart which will increase your health

You can chose between multiple players and you can chose to play at an easy, medium or hard level. The difference between these levels is primarily the damage done by enemies and how difficult it is to kill the enemies. Obviously it is easier to defeat enemies in the easy level and harder at the hard standards.

If you chose to play at the hard level of difficulty there is a type of powerup. A chicken will float around the screen and if you kill it you'll automatically progress to the next level without having to beat any of the enemies. However the chicken appears at the start of the level and quickly runs off screen.

The bigger the character you chose the slower they will in the game

All sprites came from Itch.io and none require credit
